# Creative Production Brief

## Brand Frame

`judgemebro.com` is a clean, dopamine-heavy Gen Z challenge game: one prompt, two answers, AI verdict, instant points.

The visual world should feel like a fast social challenge, not a board game or corporate AI tool.

## Preserve

- Dark high-contrast base
- Lime primary actions and reward moments
- Coral/red rivalry and judgment moments
- Cyan typing/live status
- Gold rank cues
- Real portrait-style avatars
- Kinetic light streaks
- Short punchy copy

## Avoid

- Mean roast-app energy
- Heavy gamer clutter
- Corporate SaaS dashboards
- Board game metaphors
- Dense explanatory text
- AI-credit language on viewer submissions

## Launch Asset Directions

### Ranked Duel

Show the faceoff: two avatars, same dilemma, timer, hidden answers, and a bold `Submit to Judge` button.

Best channels: TikTok launch clips, app-store screenshots, landing hero, paid social.

### Get Judged, Get Points

Use the big headline with lime reward language, rank badge, battles left, streak, and category tiles.

Best channels: homepage, social share cards, signup/onboarding, Discord/community posts.

### Streamer Room

Show the streamer dashboard with viewer answers and a selected answer blown up for stream.

Best channels: creator outreach, Twitch overlays, YouTube Shorts demos, streamer onboarding.

## Next Creative Production Move

Create a digital-product placement exploration for three surfaces:

- mobile ranked battle
- mobile result/share verdict
- desktop streamer room

Each image should keep the app UI central and avoid fake endorsements, fake pricing claims, and over-dense text.
